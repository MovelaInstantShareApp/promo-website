1. Safety Standards

Drivers must:

  • Follow all traffic laws
  • Maintain a clean vehicle
  • Avoid distracted driving
  • Ensure passengers are safely seated before starting a trip

Passengers must:

  • Respect driver property
  • Avoid damaging vehicles
  • Follow basic safety instructions during the ride

2. Illegal Activities

The platform must not be used for illegal activities such as:

  • Transporting illegal goods
  • Drug-related activities
  • Criminal behavior

Any suspected illegal activity may be reported to law enforcement authorities.

3. Identity Verification

Movela prioritizes passenger and driver safety.

Drivers must verify their identity and submit official documentation during onboarding.

Movela may perform periodic identity checks.

4. Ride Tracking

All rides are tracked via GPS through the application for safety and operational purposes.

Ride data may be stored for security, dispute resolution, and regulatory compliance.

5. Emergency Assistance

The platform may include emergency features such as:

  • Emergency contact button
  • Ride sharing with trusted contacts
  • Driver and passenger rating system

Users are encouraged to report unsafe situations immediately.
