About Movela

We are the Albanian Ride and Taxi Service Application

Movela was built with a single vision: to revolutionize the way people move across Albania and Kosova. We are a proudly local technology platform dedicated to connecting passengers with reliable, safe, and independent drivers.

Whether you need a quick ride across the city, a reliable taxi service for your daily commute, or a convenient carpooling option to travel between cities, Movela is designed to meet your every need.

Our Mission

We aim to bring seamless, affordable, and high-quality transportation to everyone. By empowering independent drivers and prioritizing passenger safety, we are not just providing rides—we are building a trusted community that moves together.
